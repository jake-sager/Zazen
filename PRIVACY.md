# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** January 20, 2026

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

- **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account to use Zazen.
- **Local Storage:** All your meditation history, streaks, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage systems (UserDefaults). 
- **No Analytics:** Zazen does not track how you use the app or send any usage data to third-party servers.

## Third-Party Services

Zazen does not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising networks.

## Your Consent

By using Zazen, you consent to this privacy policy.
